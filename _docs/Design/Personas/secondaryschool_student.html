Scenarios

Persona is working on an interesting school project, where he needs to create a logo from scratch. He hates drawing by hand, so he thinks to look for an alternative solution. While surfing the web he finds this drawing application that allows him to draw using his voice. As this is interesting, he gives it a try. After a few minutes of learning the basics he creates his logo. While presenting the project at school, his colleagues and his teacher are pretty surprised about the new logo. When he is asked on how he created it, he proudly describes them the cool application that he discovered.
